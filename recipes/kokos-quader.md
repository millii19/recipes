# Kokos Quader
* takes about 45 minutes
* for 30 pieces

## ingredients 
* 3 Eggs
* 160g powdered sugar
* 120ml milk
* 120ml oil
* 300g flour
* 1pkg backpulver

For the icing:
* 170g baking chocolate
* 220g coconut fat
* 160g powdered sugar
* 1tsp rum

For rolling:
* 120g coconut flakes

## steps
1. separate eggs
2. whip egg whites
3. combine all other ingredients
